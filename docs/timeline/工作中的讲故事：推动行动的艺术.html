“如何成为人” "HOW TO BE HUMAN"

决策过程 Decision Process

  • 由我们的边缘系统控制， Controlled by our limbic system,
  • 情绪在那里产生和处理。 Emotions are generated and processed there.
  • 决策是一个情感过程。 Decision-making is an emotional process.

工作中的讲故事 STORYTELLING AT WORK

“如果你想激励或影响他人，你确实需要在你的论证中融入情感元素。” "If you want to inspire or influence others, you absolutely need to weave emotional elements into your arguments."

例如：让人开怀大笑，让他们记住！ (e.g., make them laugh, make them remember!)

演讲的三个主要组成部分 THREE MAIN COMPONENTS OF A SPEECH

1

观众 Audience

  • 他们是谁？Who are they?
  • 他们为什么要听你说话？Why should they listen?
  • 他们能学到什么好处？What benefits can they gain?
2

演讲者 Speaker

  • 你相信吗？Do you believe it?
  • 你对你的观众有同理心？Do you empathize with your audience?
  • 做好准备，减少干扰。Be prepared, reduce distractions.
3

故事与叙述 Story & Narrative

  • 添加情感成分。Add emotional components.
  • 在人性和情感上的双向建立联系。Build connections on humanity and emotion.

故事的框架 STORY FRAMEWORK

发生了什么？What happened?

为什么会发生？Why did it happen?

如果什么都不改变，会发生什么？What happens if nothing changes?
